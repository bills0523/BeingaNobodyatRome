TITLE: The Trader
ROLE: merchant
START: trader_start

---
DATE: 110 B.C.
ID: trader_start
TEXT:
You are born in a town south of Rome.
You will not starve, but there isn’t spare in your family.
NEXT: trader_100

---
DATE: 100 B.C.
ID: trader_100
TEXT:
You start working as a boy.
You carry sacks.
You load carts.
You earn what you want.
As your father cannot give them to you.
NEXT: trader_96

---
DATE: 96 B.C.
ID: trader_96
TEXT:
Your father taught you how to accumulate wealth.
You learned how to contain your desire.
You learn how wealth works.
The more you accumulate, the more you can play with it.
NEXT: trader_92

---
DATE: 92 B.C.
ID: trader_92
TEXT:
You started your “business.”
You buy produce from the cheap.
You carry it to where it sells better.
Oil, fish, cloth, tools.
You are not yet a great merchant.
You earn little or nothing most of the time.
Your father supports you, not financially, but mentally.
NEXT: trader_88

---
DATE: 88 B.C.
ID: trader_88
TEXT:
War and unease threaten your trade.
Roads are less safe.
Prices are unstable.
A bad decision costs you months of gain.
You learn risks.
You learn profit.
NEXT: trader_84

---
DATE: 84 B.C.
ID: trader_84
TEXT:
You become more cautious.
You suppress your ambition.
You distinguish between gambling and trading.
NEXT: trader_80

---
DATE: 80 B.C.
ID: trader_80
TEXT:
You have enough to run an actual business.
You hire transport.
You bargain with scale.
You deal with people directly.
You are part of the business.
Your decision matters.
NEXT: trader_76

---
DATE: 76 B.C.
ID: trader_76
TEXT:
You start actions in Rome.
The city is the challenge.
The scale scares you.
What was big for the town can’t even be seen by Rome.
NEXT: trader_75

---
DATE: 75 B.C.
ID: trader_75
TEXT:
You learned more about business.
In Rome, reputation is all.
Relationships and trust didn’t come from nowhere.
You sometimes get nowhere.
But you are patient enough.
NEXT: trader_72

---
DATE: 72 B.C.
ID: trader_72
TEXT:
You stand firm in the city.
You understand what is essential in Rome.
Food, transport, supply.
Those make you not the wealthiest, but with a steady life.
NEXT: trader_68_choice

---
DATE: 68 B.C.
ID: trader_68_choice
TEXT:
You accumulated wealth.
You grew in scale.
You find politics in markets.
You learned quickly that merchants are not in charge.
People who control laws, taxes, and the city can ruin you quickly.
From here, you decide:
CHOICES:
- Enter politics => trader_politics_67
- Quit while you’re ahead => trader_quit_67

---
DATE: 67 B.C.
ID: trader_politics_67
TEXT:
You build relationships.
With a politician, with aristocrats, with the higher class.
You gift them.
You flatter them.
You place yourself lower.
You tolerate.
NEXT: trader_politics_64

---
DATE: 64 B.C.
ID: trader_politics_64
TEXT:
You know Catiline.
You learned his plan.
You decided.
This is the time you claim power.
NEXT: trader_politics_63

---
DATE: 63 B.C.
ID: trader_politics_63
TEXT:
You failed.
The shine of power blinded your father’s words.
You urged too much.
Too much so that it backfires on you.
You lost everything.
ENDING: true
ACHIEVEMENT: trader_gambler_ending

---
DATE: 67 B.C.
ID: trader_quit_67
TEXT:
You’ve accumulated enough.
Enough wealth to free you from working for the rest of your life.
You aren’t eager for power.
You stepped away from politics, as you see the dangers in it.
NEXT: trader_quit_66

---
DATE: 66 B.C.
ID: trader_quit_66
TEXT:
You returned to your town.
You bought land.
A lot of land.
You lend them.
You earn from them.
NEXT: trader_quit_63

---
DATE: 63 B.C.
ID: trader_quit_63
TEXT:
Living in a huge house, you heard what happened in the city.
You felt lucky for leaving politics.
You enjoyed your life.
You spent time with your family.
Inside your town,
You are the success.
ENDING: true
ACHIEVEMENT: trader_wise_merchant_ending
