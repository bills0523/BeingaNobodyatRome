DATE: A.D. 87

You were born in northern Italy.
Your family has warmth.
You don’t have to starve.
But you can’t expect more.

---
DATE: A.D. 104
ID: soldier_104
TEXT:
You volunteer for the army.
You felt honored to fight for Trajan.
You are strong enough.
They promise you awards.
But you know that will be far away.
NEXT: soldier_105

---
DATE: A.D. 105
ID: soldier_105
TEXT:
The training is hard.
It’s also boring and repetitive.
Physical training.
Weapon training.
You keep formation, build camp, and most importantly, obey orders.
Order is everything.
You sleep in tents, eat by rations, and share burdens.
NEXT: soldier_106

---
DATE: A.D. 106
ID: soldier_106
TEXT:
You joined the war against Dacia.
It is your first time on the battlefield.
You are excited, but a little scared.
But Rome doesn’t allow cowardice.
You didn’t fight like hero.
You hold formation, obey orders, and trust men beside you.
Victory came, less heroic, less exciting.
NEXT: soldier_107

---
DATE: A.D. 107
ID: soldier_107
TEXT:
The victory didn’t bring you much honor and awards.
Those went to emperors and generals.
You gained pretty much nothing.
But you see your peers with scars, limps, and wounds.
You didn’t feel like you were part of it, even though you were.
NEXT: soldier_111

---
DATE: A.D. 111
ID: soldier_111
TEXT:
You become a minor leader.
Still, you are under many layers of commands.
You obey orders and teach newbies to obey orders.
You are not a great man.
The real great man is above you.
All you do is listen to his order.
NEXT: soldier_123

---
DATE: A.D. 123
ID: soldier_123
TEXT:
You receive an honorable discharge.
They recognize you as a veteran.
Kids ask you about fights.
You would share with them how you all conquered.
But do you actually enjoy those?
NEXT: soldier_124

---
DATE: A.D. 124
ID: soldier_124
TEXT:
You settle in a town.
The money is not enough to keep you healthy, but it is enough to support your life.
Some lands.
A house.
More tranquil in your life.

DATE: A.D. 136
ID: soldier_136
TEXT:
You suffered from wounds in war.
They hurt you badly at night.
One day, you are tired.
Tired from scenes flashing at night.
Tired of living your life.
You closed your eyes.
Your burial was respected and honored.
